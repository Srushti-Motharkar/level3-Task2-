Overview

Position Title: Web Developer Intern
Location:Cognifyz Technologies(Remote)
Duration: 1 month

Internship Description The Web Developer Internship at Cognifyz Technologies offers a unique opportunity to gain hands-on experience in the field of web development. As an intern, you will work closely with our experienced developers, contributing to real projects and learning industry best practices. This internship is designed to provide you with the skills and knowledge needed to advance your career in web development.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in the design, development, and maintenance of websites and web applications.
  • Collaborate with the development team to implement new features and improve existing functionalities.
  • Participate in code reviews and contribute to the overall improvement of the codebase.
  • Debug and troubleshoot issues across various browsers and devices.
  • Stay up-to-date with emerging web technologies and industry trends.
  • Contribute to project documentation and provide support for technical inquiries.
